# Claude Skills Collection

A curated collection of Claude Code skills for enhancing AI-assisted workflows.

## Available Skills

### 1. Anti-AI-Slop (`/skills/anti-ai-slop/`)

A comprehensive skill for making AI-generated content sound authentically human. Based on extensive research of what makes content "read like AI" on platforms like LinkedIn and Twitter.

**Features:**
- Comprehensive list of 100+ AI-slop markers to avoid
- Structural patterns that reveal AI authorship
- Personal writing style profiling from your samples
- Before/after transformation examples
- Multiple usage modes (analyze, rewrite, write, profile)

**Use cases:**
- LinkedIn posts that don't scream "ChatGPT wrote this"
- Twitter threads with authentic voice
- Blog content that connects with readers
- Email newsletters that sound like you
- Any content where authenticity matters

## How to Use

1. Copy the skill file contents into your Claude Code conversation
2. Provide your writing samples for style matching (optional but recommended)
3. Use the appropriate mode:
   - **ANALYZE**: Get feedback on existing content
   - **REWRITE**: Transform AI-sounding text into human voice
   - **WRITE**: Create new content avoiding all AI markers
   - **PROFILE**: Build a style profile from your writing samples

## Research Sources

The Anti-AI-Slop skill was built from research including:
- Studies showing 54% of LinkedIn long-form posts are AI-generated
- Data showing AI posts get 45% less engagement
- Analysis of overused words like "delve" (50%+ usage increase since ChatGPT)
- Community feedback from Reddit, Twitter, and LinkedIn on AI tells
- Academic research on perplexity and burstiness in AI text detection
