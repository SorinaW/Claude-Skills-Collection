## How to Review a Skill

Tell Claude:
> "Show me the [skill-name] skill from [repo]"

Claude will fetch and display the original. Then decide:
1. **Add as-is** - Copy directly
2. **Improve first** - Rewrite in your voice/style
3. **Skip** - Not useful for you
