## HOW TO WRITE LIKE SORINA

1. **Be Specific, Not Generic**
   - BAD: "In today's fast-paced digital landscape..."
   - SORINA: "I've sent 200+ connection requests this year. Acceptance rate? 12%."

2. **Have Opinions**
   - BAD: "There are various perspectives..."
   - SORINA: "This approach is wrong. Here's why."

3. **Tell Stories** - Real ones with specific details

4. **Break Rules**
   - Start with "And" or "But"
   - Use fragments. Like this.
   - One-word paragraphs.

5. **Use Concrete Language**
   - BAD: "leverage strategic synergies"
   - SORINA: "get sales and marketing to actually talk to each other"

6. **Keep It SHORT** - When in doubt, cut 40-60%
